# Spezies: Hexblood

> **Hexblood**



#### Dunkelsicht

You have *[[Dunkelsicht]]* with a Reichweite of 60 feet.



#### Eerie Token

Als Bonusaktion kannst du ein magisches Token erzeugen, indem du eine Haarsträhne harmlos entfernst, einen Nagel ablöst oder andere Methoden anwendest. Solange das Token besteht, erhältst du folgende Vorteile:

- **Distant Nachricht** As a *Magische Aktion* action, you can send a telepathic Nachricht of 25 words or fewer to a creature holding or carrying the token, as long as you are within 10 miles of it.
- **Remote Viewing** If you are within 10 miles of the token, you can take a *Magische Aktion* action to extend your Sinne through the token for 1 Minute, until you have the handlungsunfähig Zustand, or until you end this state (no action required). During this state, you can see and hear from the token as if you were located where it is. When this state ends, the token is harmlessly destroyed. Unless the token is destroyed early, it lasts until you finish a Lange Rast. Once you create a token using this feature, you can't do so again until you finish a Lange Rast.
- **Verhexen magische Aktion** You always have the *Selbstverwandlung* and *Verhexen* spells prepared. You can cast each spell once without a Zauberplatz, and you regain the ability to cast it in that way when you finish a Lange Rast. You can also cast the spell using any Zauberplätze you have of the appropriate level. Intelligenz, Weisheit, or Charisma is your Zauberfähigkeit for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select this species).
